MY TRAVEL BLOG!

My name is Ali Ashraf and I am from Lahore, Pakistan and I have been living in Barcelona for 6 years. Traveling entirely changed my life, my way of looking towards world it makes me feel alive excited curious i want to know whole world i want travel whole World i want to meet new people want to know about them, their culture, society, country i want to make new friends around the world !

I didn't go outside of Catalunya for the first 5 years. I was so busy settling myself in Barcelona to get a resident card became legal , it was not easy especially when you are an immigrant and you have family back in your country to support it makes it more difficult. Well I managed to start saving some money and my first trip was The Netherlands and it changed my life. I wanted more, that's how my journey starts !

The Netherlands

My school friend who lives in Amsterdam visited me in June 2018. I showed him Barcelona. We had a great time and fun. He invited me to Amsterdam. I could not resist , so I finally visited him the same year in October . We had one more friend from Germany. It was a 4 days trip. Our first day we hung out in Amsterdam , It was so fun and the best start of the trip. Most interesting and surprising fact about Amsterdam is it is a city of museums , it's a heaven for people who love museums. You can spend the whole week just visiting them.

We visited Den Haag , which is a beautiful city. We wanted to do bungee jumps but it was a windy day and they were closed. Then i decided to walk on the beach but damn because wind , sand was everywhere in my mouth , hair even in pockets yup it was a bad decision. Rotterdam is a magnificent city. This city makes you wonder if you are in the future. I love the Netherlands. I will visit again but next time I will carry my umbrella because weather is unpredictable and it's always raining there , so you have to mentaly prepare for that. I am surprised that in Amsterdam almost everyone speaks english it's a huge benefit for travelers.

Switzerland

In June we did a Switzerland trip. My friends and I gathered in Geneva. I flew from Barcelona and my friend from Amsterdam drove all the way to Geneva. It was a 17 hours drive. Our third friend was already there. Geneva is a diplomatic city. I thought Geneva was the capital but nope. Bern is the capital I was not expecting but Bern is beautiful capital city. Its nature surprised you, it felt like heaven but it's just a beginning.

Switzerland surprised me Words are not enough, it was Magic ! we continued and our next stop was Lucerne , we celebrated my friend's birthday on Lucerne Lake, it was the best birthday celebration ever, we did swimming after. Interlaken is the winner ! you will find your thrill adventure fun and peace. We wind up our trip and Zurich was our last stop. We did couchsurfing and it was a wonderful experience knowing locals and learning about their culture and cities. It was a huge help and no doubt they are big hearted and wonderful hosts.
